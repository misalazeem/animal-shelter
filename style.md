## 4. Design Elements (IQ Optimization)

### **The "Simon" Badge**
Since Simon is the origin, I recommend creating a small vector illustration of Simon (perhaps a silhouette) that appears in the Footer or as a watermark on the site. He is the "Patron Saint" of the brand.

### **Typography Tweak**
* **Quotes:** Use *Italics* in **Merriweather** for Hailey’s direct quotes ("sink or swim time"). This emphasizes her voice distinct from the brand voice.

### **Color Application**
* **Donation Progress Bars:** If you run specific fundraisers (e.g., "Simon's Medical Fund"), use the **Timber Gold** to fill the bar. It looks like "filling the tree" or "growth."
